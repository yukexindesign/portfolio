How to create an explanatory video that is both informative and engaging at the same time?

Financial concepts are typically complex and hard to digest. Visualising the budget process, government revenue and expenditure projections in a clear and entertaining manner proved to be a challenge. The goal is to grab the audience's attention from the beginning and maintain it till the end.

Approach

One element appears at one time to minimise distraction and convey the meaning effectively.

Create and maintain flow between each scenes to convey connections and meaning while keeping the video engaging.

Utilize vibrant graphics to create patterns and trends while sustaining attention.
