Ineffective branding of an information overloaded site plagued with monetization issues

Home page before revamp

Being part of Business Times has gives its Lifestyle counterpart a serious and businessy impression, resulting in a lack of exposure to a larger audience and potential advertisers.

Besides, the huge amount of content accompanied by an ineffective interface and information architecture resulted in difficulty navigating through the web pages to find the desired content.

How can we enhance discoverability of lifestyle content in a business news website?

  • An interface that encourages users to explore the site and discover more content.
  • Refreshed image and content that appeals to the general, young and active, without alienating the affluent older crowd.

Modern interface with clearer visual hierachy

Improved information architecture:
Categorising content into "theme" and "type"

Articles can carry multiple tags of 1 theme and 1 type - Better inform user it's categorisation while providing link points to each subsequent category to browse more related content,
eg. STYLE + INTERVIEW

Theme

F&B / ARTS / MOVIES / MUSIC / DESIGN / STYLE / TRAVEL / WELLNESS / CARS / GADGETS

Type

FEATURE / INTERVIEW / CITY BUZZ / REVIEW

Better arrangement of related useful information

Seperate placement of details such as restaurant location and movie showtimes at the side column which tracks along as the user scroll for easy reference

Cards that accomodate both portrait and landscape images of all aspect ratio
